About

I build with intention — not just to ship, but to last.

Hey, I am Jonathan — a software engineer based in Cairo with a passion for building things that actually matter. I've always been drawn to the intersection of logic and problem-solving — for me, writing code isn't just about making things work, it's about understanding why they work and engineering them to last. I don't reach for the first solution that fits; I think through the details, the edge cases, the decisions that will still make sense six months after launch. Hard problems don't slow me down — they push me to think differently and find the approach that actually fits. While my day-to-day revolves around building full-stack web systems and enterprise platforms, I'm constantly pushing deeper — into system architecture, computer science fundamentals, and how well-engineered software holds up under real-world pressure.
